Product making steps

Step 1: Embroider the punch needle with 1mm wool and the smallest needle on the fabric according to the pre-printed drawing, leaving the hands and feet unembroidered.

Step 2: Cut the fabric along the dashed line

Step 3: Sew the finished product Step 4: Stuff and finish

Notes when making products

  • -  It is recommended to draft out the excess fabric before embroidering the product , to avoid being damaged, having to remove it many times, causing damage to the fabric. If the embroidery is wrong and needs to be removed and redone, you can gently scratch the fabric with your hand so that the fabric does not leave too large a hole.
  • -  Apply glue to the reverse side after embroidery is complete, and wait for the glue to dry completely before sewing to avoid sticking during stitching.
  • -  The product uses fabric glue, can be washed with normal water . Should be washed by hand gently and should not be washed too many times, it will cause the wool to ruffle.
